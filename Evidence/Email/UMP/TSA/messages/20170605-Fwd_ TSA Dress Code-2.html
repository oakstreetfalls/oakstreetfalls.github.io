TSA Dress Code

As you continue to make plans to attend the 2017 national TSA conference, please be aware that all conference participants, including guests, parents, and children, must adhere to the national TSA dress code. Anyone who is not in compliance with the general session dress code will not be allowed to enter. 

Be sure to share this information with all parents and guests accompanying your chapter so they are aware of the national TSA dress code. 

All conference attendees must register to attend the conference and wear a conference identification badge at all times. Only registered conference attendees will be admitted to all general sessions, including the awards ceremony on Sunday, June 25th. 

Thank you for making plans to attend the 2017 national TSA conference from June 21st to June 25th at the Rosen Shingle Creek Resort in Orlando, Florida.
