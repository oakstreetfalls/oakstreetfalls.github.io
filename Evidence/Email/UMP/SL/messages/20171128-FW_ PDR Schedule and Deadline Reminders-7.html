Foreign National Contact Information Request

The majority of activities during launch week will take place in public areas but we will offer tours of facilities and labs at NASA’s Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC).  MSFC is located on an active US Army base and is a controlled access location.  Any Foreign National wishing to participate in tours of MSFC will need to provide information to be used in a security screening.  Because this information is private and sensitive, we will coordinate the screening process directly with the Foreign National or their parent/guardian.  Please complete the attached “FN contact info” document and return it to Zach (zachary.a.koch@nasa.gov) by November 3rd, 2017.  Only those Foreign Nationals who are interested in participating in launch week tours need to provide contact info at this time.

 

For our purposes, a Foreign National is defined as anyone who is NOT a US citizen, this would include those who have US Permanent Resident status (i.e. a Green Card), an Employment Authorization Card, or are in the US on a VISA.

 

During Launch Week, ONLY FOREIGN NATIONALS WHO HAVE BEEN APPROVED for tours should participate in the tours of MSFC.  All Foreign Nationals are welcome to participate in activities occurring outside of MSFC.
