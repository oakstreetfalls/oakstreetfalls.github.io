Teams,

 

Please read the following information regarding launch week.

 

LRRs and Check In/Ticket Sale tables will be located on the 2nd floor of the Embassy Suites hotel.  LRRs are located inside both the Redstone and Madison Rooms, and tables will be located right outside the rooms.

 

Weather for Saturday is looking great for a launch, and we will keep you informed about any changes.  Tomorrow, however, we will have strong thunderstorms across northern Alabama that could create some delays for traveling.  Please be aware of this, and stay safe during your travels.

 

Please remember that the Awards Ceremony on Saturday is, at a minimum, business casual, and you are free to dress up more if you wish.  When you arrive at the Davidson Center, one of us will hand the team their participation plaque, and we will have a photographer ready to take team photos.  There are no assigned seats for teams during dinner, but please do not sit in the reserved seating for VIPs at the front of the building.  Following dinner, we will have a few speakers present, and end by announcing SL related awards (excluding overall and rookie awards).

 

Thank you, and I look forward to meeting you all tomorrow!
