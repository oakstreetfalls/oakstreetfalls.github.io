Launch Week Hotel Information

We are pleased to announce that we will use the Embassy Suites in downtown Huntsville as our host hotel this year.  Teams are not required to stay at the host hotel but are required to attend meetings, hardware checks, and various other launch week activities occurring at the Embassy Suites.  Buses for Marshall tours and to the launch field will pick up and drop off at the Embassy Suites.  Rooms at the Embassy Suites are available to teams at a group rate of $91 plus taxes per night.  All rooms are suites containing a bedroom with either 1 King or 2 Queen beds and a separate living room area that contains a sofa bed.  A complimentary breakfast is included and the hotel has an airport shuttle available (please contact hotel directly for shuttle info).  Parking is free for hotel guests.  Reservations can be made via the link or phone contact information included below.  Rooms are limited so please book early and only reserve what your team will use.  The group rate is only good through March 5th, 2017.

Team Mentor Documentation Request

We will be providing team mentors with a launch week travel stipend.  The $1,000 stipend will be issued in the form of a check which will be distributed during launch week.  In order to receive the check at launch week, each mentor must submit a completed and signed W-9 and a copy of their NAR or TRA membership card by January 13, 2017.  A blank W-9 is attached.  The completed W-9 will contain Personally Identifying Information (PII) and must be submitted in a secure fashion.

Instructions for submission are included below.  If your NAR/TRA card will expire prior to launch week, please submit your current card and send an updated card upon renewal.  Please direct any questions to Fred.

 

Email submission instructions:

If the W-9 is submitted by email, it MUST be encrypted and the password must be sent in a separate email.  If using Adobe Acrobat, encryption settings can be selected in Adobe Acrobat under “File – Properties – Security – Security Method”.  This method only works within Acrobat if you have scanned your completed document.  If you do not have access to Adobe Acrobat, encryption can be accomplished by several free software programs such as WinZip or WinRAR.  The SBU form should be included in your email but does not need to be encrypted.  Email your documents and password in separate emails to Fred.Kepner@nasa.gov.

 

Fax submission instructions:

Fax submissions should be sent to Fred Kepner at 256.544.2511.  Please do not fax submissions between December 21st and 28th as he will be on Christmas leave.  Include the attached SBU cover sheet as the first page of the fax.  The only information that needs to be on the cover sheet is your name and your team/school, in the appropriate spots on the bottom of the form.  If faxing your documents, please take care to ensure that your certification card is readable.  Due to the images on the cards, you may need to lighten the scan in order for it to be legible.

Foreign National Contact Information Request

Foreign Nationals (FNs) are defined as anyone who does not have US Citizenship.  This includes, but is not limited to, those who are in the United States on a foreign VISA, hold Lawful Permanent Resident status (i.e. Green Card), or have an Employment Authorization card (EAD, Form I-765).  Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC) is located on a secure military facility and all FNs must complete a background screening prior to being allowed to participate in the optional MSFC tours.  Teams must identify any Foreign Nationals wishing to participate in launch week tours by January 13th, 2017.  Each Foreign National seeking participation in tours will be required to complete a detailed information request and provide color copies of specific documents such as passports/VISAs/etc.  Due to the private nature of the information collected, collection of these documents/information will be done directly with the FN team member.  Communication regarding documents is also permitted with a parent/guardian if the team member is a minor.

 

Team leads are asked to communicate this request to their entire team and send a list of their team’s Foreign Nationals, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and countries of citizenship, to Fred Kepner by the January 13th deadline, however early submissions are appreciated.  Email FN info to Fred.Kepner@nasa.gov.
